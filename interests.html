My Interests

  • Crochet I first learned to crochet from my grandmother so it's something that we can talk about together. I have made a few different stuffed animals but clothing like cardigans are my favorite things to make.
  • Reading My favorite books that I have read this year are The Priory of the Organge Tree, Babel and Legend Born. My favorite genre is fantasy.
  • Sewing I have always enjoyed sewing after I learned from my mother. I recently got a Singer VS 2 treadle sewing machine that I cleaned up. It was made in the 1890s.
  • Historical Fashion I love learning about fashion history and how changes in society changed fashion and vice versa. My favorite time period has to be the 1890-1905.
  • Baking My mother also taught me how to bake. She mostly taught me how to bake bread including sourdough and focaccia. But I also enjoy baking desserts such as cookies and tarts.
